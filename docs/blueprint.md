# **App Name**: Atlas Health Clinic

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Landing page with a hero section, clinic intro, quick info cards (location, timings, phone), service previews, Google rating highlight, patient testimonials, and call-to-action buttons (Call Now, Get Directions).
- About Us Page: Details about the clinic's story, mission, values, patient-first philosophy, and ethical healthcare promise, presented in a friendly and human tone. Sections include Who we are, Our mission, and Why patients trust us.
- Services Page: Comprehensive list of services offered with clear, non-technical descriptions, including service name, short explanation, who itâ€™s for, and when to visit. Each service is represented by a card with an icon.
- Doctors Page: Individual profiles for each doctor including their name, specialization, experience summary, and care philosophy. Optional doctor photo placeholder and languages spoken.
- Patient Reviews Page: Showcase of static Google review quotes with star ratings to build trust and credibility. Features clean testimonial cards and a trust-focused design.
- Contact Us Page: Detailed contact information including full address, phone number with click-to-call functionality, Google Maps embed, and opening hours table. Includes a call button (mobile) and clear layout.
- FAQ Page: Accordion layout to display frequently asked questions (e.g., Do I need an appointment?, What are clinic hours?, Do you accept walk-ins?, Where are you located?), improving SEO and user experience.
- Footer: Footer section that appears on every page, containing the clinic name, address, phone number, opening hours, quick links, and copyright information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#ADD8E6) for a calming and trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF) to maintain a clean and professional aesthetic.
- color: Accent color: A slightly darker shade of blue (#77B5FE) used for call-to-action buttons and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, providing a modern and neutral aesthetic.
- Use clean, modern line icons to represent services and enhance visual appeal.
- Employ a responsive grid layout to ensure optimal viewing experience across all devices.
- Incorporate smooth transitions and hover effects to enhance user engagement and create a modern feel.
